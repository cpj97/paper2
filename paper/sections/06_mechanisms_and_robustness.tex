\section{Mechanisms and Robustness}

\subsection{Mechanisms}

Use this section only if you have credible mechanism evidence. Otherwise keep it short and relabel as ``Additional Evidence''.

Possible mechanism outcomes:

\begin{itemize}
    \item changes in the level of annual loss,
    \item changes in persistence of loss,
    \item differential effects by initial pressure,
    \item interactions with institutional or geographic constraints.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Robustness Checks}

Likely checks to consider:

\begin{itemize}
    \item alternative overlap thresholds,
    \item dropping cells treated before the panel start,
    \item alternative treatment aggregations,
    \item excluding overlapping treatment types,
    \item restricting the sample to comparable support,
    \item placebo treatment dates,
    \item alternative estimators for staggered treatment timing.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Threats to Interpretation}

Be explicit about the limits of the design. This usually makes the paper stronger.
